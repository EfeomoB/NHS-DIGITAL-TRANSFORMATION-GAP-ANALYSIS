## ğŸ’¡ Key Findings & Recommended Solutions

### 1. Unified Digital Patient Record System  
Consolidate records across the Integrated Care System (ICS) into a single shared care record.  
**Benefits:** Better decision-making, fewer duplicated tests, reduced admin workload.

### 2. Automate GP-to-Hospital Referrals  
Leverage GP Connect & NHS e-Referral Service (eRS) for seamless, digital-first referrals.  
**Benefits:** Reduced manual processing and faster referral-to-treatment times.

### 3. Digital Training for Clinicians  
Provide ongoing upskilling, digital literacy programmes, and digital champions.  
**Benefits:** Higher adoption, reduced burnout, improved data quality.

### 4. Increase Patient Digital Engagement  
Deploy a digital inclusion strategy to expand awareness of tools like the NHS App.  
**Benefits:** Greater patient autonomy and reduced demand on phone-based services.

### 5. Cloud-First Infrastructure  
Modernise backend systems by adopting cloud hosting and retiring outdated legacy systems.  
**Benefits:** Scalability, resilience, and enhanced security.

### 6. RPA & AI Adoption  
Automate repetitive administrative workflows and pilot AI-assisted diagnostics.  
**Benefits:** Increased efficiency and more time for direct clinical care.

### 7. Remote Monitoring & Virtual Wards  
Enable remote care for chronic conditions and post-discharge patients.  
**Benefits:** Reduced hospital admissions and improved patient outcomes.

### 8. Patient-Initiated Follow-Up (PIFU)  
Allow patients to manage follow-ups digitally based on their needs.  
**Benefits:** Better experience and optimised clinic capacity.
